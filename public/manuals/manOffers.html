On the OFFERS page you can download files, containing our offers, separated by lead time

For your convenience, we have two kinds of offer files:

  • FULL files, containing items of all brands available in meaning lead time
  • BRAND files, containing only items of appropriate brand in meaning lead time

All files have the same structure as follows:

  • BRAND - unique code of the manufacturer in our system (* presents only in FULL files)
  • PARTNUMBER - manufacturer's unique item number
  • PRICE (USD) - price in USD from our warehouse, without further delivery (1USD =3.65 AED)
  • CATALOG - unique logo of the supplier
  • RATING - supply quality statistics
  • QUANTITY – available quantity (is empty for "by order" items)
  • WEIGHT - physical weight of the item in KG
  • VOLUME - Volume weight of the item in KG
Each file also contains Delivery costs in appropriate columns:
  • EKBA (Ekaterinburg, RUSSIA)
  • KIVA (Kiev, UKRAINE)
  • MSKA (Moscow, RUSSIA)

To calculate your final price at the particular destination, you need to add the delivery cost to the PRICE (USD)
*if you have any special discount, please note, that discount applies only for PRICE (USD) and doesn’t apply for the delivery cost
